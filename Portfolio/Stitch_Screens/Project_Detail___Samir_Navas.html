Form & Void: A Typographic Exploration of Negative Space.

The "Form & Void" poster series challenges the conventional boundaries of typographic expression by treating negative space as the primary communicative element. Commissioned for the inaugural exhibition at Obscura Gallery, the project strips away extraneous ornamentation to focus purely on structural tension.

Utilizing a rigorous 12-column grid system, each piece in the series explores the delicate balance between high-contrast black ink and pristine off-white substrate. The resulting aesthetic is uncompromisingly modern, commanding attention through its stark minimalism and precise execution.
